About us

Kathu born.
Sun powered.

Why we do this

Kathu sits under some of the strongest, most reliable sunshine on the planet — while its people pay more every year for power that switches off without warning. That never made sense to us.

Agri Electronix installs solar and backup power for the people who keep this region running: households tired of load shedding schedules, farmers who need water moving whether Eskom shows up or not, and businesses that can't afford dark hours.

We're local, we size systems honestly, and — the thing our clients mention most — we're still here after the install. When you call, we answer. When something needs attention, we come out. That's the whole philosophy.

How we work

Four steps.
Zero surprises.

1 · Assess

Send us your electricity bill on WhatsApp or book a free site visit. We look at usage, roof and goals.

2 · Design

A system sized to your real consumption, with clear numbers: cost, savings, payback period.

3 · Install

Clean workmanship, quality hardware, compliance certificate. Most homes done in a day or two.

4 · Support

After-sales care that actually exists. Monitoring, maintenance, and a phone that gets answered.
